Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join QBridge courses?

Any late middle or high school student interested in quantum computing who has completed Algebra II or equivalent can join.

Is any prior programming experience required?

No prior programming experience is required; we teach all necessary coding as part of the course.

How much does it cost?

All our programs are completely free.

Will there be certificates?

Yes, students who complete the course will receive a certificate of completion.

How do I register?

Simply go to the registration page and fill out the form to sign up.
