Curriculum Outline

Week 1-2: Orientation & Foundations

Program introduction, setting expectations, and establishing foundational knowledge. Introduction to core concepts and tools.

Week 3-4: Core Concepts

Deep dive into fundamental principles of the subject area. Interactive lectures and group discussions.

Week 5-6: Applied Learning

Hands-on projects and case studies. Students begin applying concepts to real-world scenarios.

Week 7-8: Advanced Topics

Exploration of more complex material with guest lectures from field experts.

Week 9: Capstone Preparation

Students begin work on final projects with instructor guidance and peer collaboration.

Week 10: Presentations & Graduation

Final project presentations, feedback sessions, and program completion celebration.

Program Structure Details

  • 📅 Duration: 10 weeks per program
  • ⏱️ Time Commitment: 4-6 hours per week (2 live sessions + independent work)
  • 👥 Class Size: Limited to 25 students per cohort for personalized attention
  • 🏆 Certification: Digital certificate upon successful completion
  • 🤝 Community: Access to alumni network and mentorship opportunities
