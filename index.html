Welcome to qBridge!

We host free live courses designed specifically for late middle school and high school students to learn about quantum computing.

Explore Programs View Curriculum

Why Choose QBridge?

📖

Prior Experience

Our courses are designed for students who have completed Algebra II. All other concepts, such as physics, coding, and linear algebra, will be taught as part of the course.

✏️

Live Interactive Sessions

Both our virtual and in-person programs have live sessions, letting students communicate with their instructors as well as their peers.

💰

Completely Free

All of our programs are completely free, and we offer a certificate of completion to all students who complete our programs!

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join QBridge courses?

Any late middle or high school student interested in quantum computing who has completed Algebra II or equivalent can join.

Is any prior programming experience required?

No prior programming experience is required; we teach all necessary coding as part of the course.

How much does it cost?

All our programs are completely free.

Will there be certificates?

Yes, students who complete the course will receive a certificate of completion.

How do I register?

Simply go to the registration page and fill out the form to sign up.
